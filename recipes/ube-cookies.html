Ube Crinkle Cookies

Source: Lalaine Manalo of kawalingpinoy.com

Ube Crinkles are soft, moist, and bursting with Ube flavors. These classic cookies with a tropical twist are easy to make and perfect for coffee or tea time.

-Lalaine Manalo

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups ube jam
  • 2 teaspoon ube extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, cream the sugar and vegetable oil.
  2. Add the egg and beat until frothy and color becomes pale.
  3. Add the ube jam and ube extract and beat again until well blended.
  4. Gradually add the flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix using a spatula or electric mixer with dough attachment. The dough will be very sticky. Add violet food color, if desired, to achieve a deep purple color.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. Alternatively, place in the freezer for at least 2 hours.
  6. Scoop a spoonful, 1 to 2-inch in diameter, of chilled dough and drop into a bowl of powdered sugar. Roll dough in powdered sugar to thoroughly coat. Roll the dough balls in powdered sugar once more, for a total of two times.
  7. Arrange at least 2 inches apart on a baking sheet.
  8. Bake in a 350 F for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from oven and let the cookies stand for 1-2 minutes on the baking sheet and then transfer to a cooling rack.

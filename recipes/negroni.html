Negroni

Speculated to have originated in Florence, Italy, the Negroni is a simple cocktail made up of 3 different liquors in equal parts. Taste the bitterness of the citrus undertones with this pre-dinner drink!

Ingredients

  • 1 part Campari
  • 1 part sweet red vermouth
  • 1 part gin
  • ice
  • orange peel for garnish

Directions

  1. Combine equal parts Campari, sweet red vermouth, and gin into a cocktail glass.
  2. Top drink with ice and stir.
  3. Take an orange peel and squeeze it over a flame into the glass.
